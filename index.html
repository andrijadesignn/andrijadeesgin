FAQ

Quick answers before starting a project or merch order.

How do I start a design project?

Send a short message with the type of project, references, deadline and where the design will be used. I will reply with the next steps and what I need from you.

What files do I receive?

Depending on the project, final delivery can include PNG, JPG, PDF and print-ready files. Logo and identity work can also be prepared for digital and print use.

How much does a project cost?

Visual identity starts from 120 EUR, print and editorial design from 60 EUR, merch and social graphics from 35 EUR, and photography support from 40 EUR. Final price depends on scope, deadline and delivery files.

How does merch ordering work?

You choose the product, size, quantity and delivery details. The order is then confirmed manually so availability, shipping and payment details are clear before production.

Can I request custom merch graphics?

Yes. Custom artwork can be adapted for hoodies, shirts, badges, posters and other print formats while keeping the design sharp and usable.

Where can I contact you fastest?

Instagram and Fiverr are the fastest options for direct communication. Behance is best for viewing the complete portfolio.
